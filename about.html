ABOUT RED RIDERS SPORTS CLUB

Red Riders sports club is one of the newest and most recognized sports club in Bangalore. We deliver the best ambience and services for adventurous sports such as Go-karting, Paint-ball, Zip-lining, gunshots and so much more. It is located in Sarjapur where the connection between nature and adventure is closely felt.
